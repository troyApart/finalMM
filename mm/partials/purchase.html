What can you do with Micro Manager?
There are two types of users for Micro Manager

Micro User:

  • Join or leave a group
  • Update task’s progress
  • Post a comment on a message board

With an upgraded account you can access much more of Micro Manager

Pro User:

  • Create, delete, join, or leave a group
  • Add, delete, and assign tasks
  • Update task’s status and edit task’s properties
  • Can delete own completed tasks
  • Edit other member’s privileges
  • Post, remove, and edit a thread
  • Post and remove messages
  • Delete other's messages
